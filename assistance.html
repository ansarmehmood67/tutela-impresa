FAQs & Technical Support

How do I register on the platform?

Go to the homepage, click Register, and follow the step-by-step form. Verify your email to complete the process.

How can I upload my documents securely?

Log in, go to 'My Documents', and use the drag-and-drop uploader. All files are encrypted and stored safely.

Can I get a 1-on-1 consultation?

Yes! Book a consultation from your dashboard or email us at consulting@tutela-impresa.com.

What if I have technical issues?

Reach our technical team via WhatsApp or email, or consult the Troubleshooting Guide under 'Resources'.
